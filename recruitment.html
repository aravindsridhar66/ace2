Recruitment

How to join? Come check this page during first few weeks of a new quarter for updated events calendar or follow us on Facebook to learn about our recruitment information!

Who can join?

  • Individuals who demonstrate enthusiasm in carrying out our mission to form a socially and academically collaborative network
  • Individuals who have and maintain at least a 2.5 GPA
  • University of California,San Diego Jacobs School of Engineering major affiliates (this includes Computer Science as well)
